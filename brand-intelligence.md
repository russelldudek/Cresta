# Cresta brand intelligence

Observed: 2026-07-13  
Purpose: document the recognition layer for an independent candidate vision.

## Identity

The official Cresta wordmark is committed locally at `assets/brand/cresta-logo.svg`. It was retrieved from Cresta's current production Webflow CDN:

`https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/67fe49bf21b9f9d5b910d3c9/68653f17c341bd5245e35bb7_CRESTA-logo.svg`

The asset remains unmodified apart from the addition of an accessible SVG title. It must be displayed at its natural 106:22 proportion. It is paired above the fold with `Independent candidate vision by Russell Dudek`; the campaign never presents itself as an official Cresta publication.

## Color

Cresta's current production stylesheet exposes the following variables:

- Wave `#205AE3`
- Light Blue `#A8C5FF`
- Deep Sea `#25252A`
- Dusk `#3D3D47`
- Cool Neutral `#F4F6F9`
- Foam `#EBF0F5`

Source stylesheet:

`https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/67fe49bf21b9f9d5b910d3c9/css/cresta-staging.shared.979e3f866.min.css`

Wave blue establishes recognition. Deep Sea and neutral fields carry most text and layout. Light blue, foam, light orange, and light purple support state distinctions inside the candidate-original Activation Boundary.

## Typography

Current official CSS identifies DM Sans Variable for headings and Inter Variable for body text. DM Mono is delivered through Google Fonts for technical labels. The campaign uses licensed web delivery and explicit Arial, system sans, and system monospace fallbacks. No proprietary font files are copied.

## Geometry and motion

Official pages use restrained rounded fields, pale technical surfaces, crisp dark typography, blue action color, and soft wave or signal geometry. The campaign translates those cues into an original responsive boundary separating `Capability shipped` from `Outcome realized`.

Motion is candidate-original and role-derived: scenario packets are absorbed by a weak condition or cross the boundary after the user repairs it. It is not a recreation of Cresta product UI, the Agent Operations Center, or an official deployment workflow.

## Asset-use decision

Only the official wordmark is required. Public customer logos and product screenshots are intentionally omitted because the operating argument is clearer through original diagrams and source-linked results. This also prevents the campaign from resembling a copied marketing page.
